# Unstuffed Peppers

## Ingredients

- 4 measures of rice cooked with beef bullion

- 2 lbs of ground beef

- About 10 Bell Peppers

- Various seasonings to taste: Onion, Garlic, Season Salt

- 1 Jar of Pasta Sauce (Prego/Ragu)

- 4 Cups of shredded cheese (Fiesta blend, or anything will do)

## Directions

Saute peppers, onion, garlic, and beef while rice is cooking.

Stir in the rice and mix well.

Add 1 jar of pasta sauce (Prego) and cheese while still hot (or continue cooking
until everything is melted)
